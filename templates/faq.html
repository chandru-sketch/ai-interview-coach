Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using the AI Interview Coach platform.

How does the AI Interview Coach work?
It uses advanced AI to simulate real interview scenarios and provide feedback.
Can I upload my resume?
Yes, upload your resume in PDF, DOC, or DOCX format for personalized feedback.
Is my data private?
Your data is secure and used only for improving your interview experience.
What types of interviews can I practice?
You can practice technical, HR, behavioral, and project-based interviews tailored to your field and experience level.
How do I start a mock interview?
Click the "Start Mock Interview" button on the home page to begin a session with AI-generated questions and feedback.
Can I get feedback on my answers?
Yes, after each answer, the AI provides constructive feedback to help you improve your responses.
What if I don't know the answer to a question?
You can skip questions or ask the AI for hints and suggestions to guide your learning.
How do I download my resume as a PDF?
Use the Resume Maker feature to create and download a professional PDF version of your resume.
Is there a limit to how many interviews I can practice?
No, you can practice as many interviews as you like to build your confidence and skills.
Who can I contact for support or feedback?
You can use the Feedback section on the home page to share your experience or contact the support team for assistance.
Can I practice for specific job roles?
Yes, you can select your field and add tags to focus on specific job roles or skills during your mock interviews.
Does the AI support behavioral and technical questions?
Absolutely! The AI can ask both behavioral and technical questions, adapting to your chosen field and difficulty level.
How do I track my interview progress?
Your dashboard shows stats like answered questions, correct answers, and completed mock interviews to help you monitor your progress.
Can I use the platform on mobile devices?
Yes, the website is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
What happens if I lose my internet connection?
Your progress is saved automatically, but you may need to refresh the page and log in again once your connection is restored.
How do I reset my password?
Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to receive a reset code and set a new password.
Can I get tips for improving my resume?
Yes, after uploading your resume, the AI provides suggestions and highlights missing sections to help you improve your document.
Is there a video guide for interview tips?
Yes, check the home page for a short video with expert interview tips and best practices.
How do I report a bug or technical issue?
Use the Feedback form or contact support to report any bugs or issues you encounter.
Can I customize the difficulty of my interview questions?
Yes, you can select the difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard) before starting your mock interview.
Does the AI support multiple languages?
Currently, the platform supports English. More languages may be added in the future.
How do I log out of my account?
Click the "Logout" button in the menu bar to securely log out of your account.
Can I use the AI Interview Coach for free?
Yes, you can access core features for free. Some advanced features may require a subscription in the future.
How do I update my profile information?
Go to your profile page to update your bio, skills, phone number, and upload a new resume or avatar.
Will my interview data be shared with employers?
No, your interview data is private and will not be shared with third parties or employers.
